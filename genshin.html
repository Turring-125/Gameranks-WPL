Genshin Impact

PS, Xbox, PC, mobile

User Score:

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. Set in the fantastical world of Teyvat, players take on the role of the Traveler, a mysterious individual searching for their lost sibling while unraveling the secrets of the seven elemental regions of Teyvat. Each region is governed by a different element and is influenced by unique cultural themes.

The game features an expansive, visually stunning open world, with diverse environments ranging from lush forests and towering mountains to bustling cities and deep caverns. Players can explore, solve puzzles, battle enemies, and unlock powerful characters, each with unique elemental abilities, combat styles, and elemental affinities. Combat in Genshin Impact is fast-paced and dynamic, blending real-time action with elemental interaction to create a fluid and strategic gameplay experience.

With a deep story, regular updates, a wide range of playable characters, and a gacha-based system for unlocking new heroes and weapons, Genshin Impact offers a constantly evolving world that appeals to both casual players and hardcore fans alike. Available on multiple platforms, including PC, PlayStation, and mobile devices, Genshin Impact has gained a massive global following since its release.
